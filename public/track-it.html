Project 2: TrackIt - Health and Fitness Made Easy

TrackIt is a fitness-tracking app that helps users achieve their health goals with real-time activity tracking, goal setting, and workout plans. It syncs with popular wearable devices, enabling users to monitor their fitness progress across platforms.

Key Features

  • Real-time tracking of steps, workouts, and calories.
  • Integration with Fitbit, Apple Watch, and Garmin.
  • Goal setting and visual progress charts.
  • Curated workout plans for all fitness levels.
  • Daily health tips and motivational insights.

Technology Stack

  • Android, iOS
  • Swift, Kotlin
  • Google Fit, Apple HealthKit

TrackIt empowers users to take control of their fitness journey, keeping them motivated and on track.

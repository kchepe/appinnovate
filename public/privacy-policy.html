Privacy Policy

Your project and personal information are always safe with us.

AppInnovate Solutions is committed to providing you with the best possible experience in custom app development, UI/UX design, and app maintenance and support. AppInnovate Solutions abides by the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), which outlines principles regarding the privacy of our customers.

Collection of your personal information

Many aspects of our website can be accessed without providing personal information. However, to request a quote or contact us, you may be required to submit personally identifiable information.

Sharing of Your Personal Information

Your personal information will not be shared with other organizations unless collaboration is necessary to provide you with services. In such cases, we will seek your explicit permission before sharing your information. AppInnovate Solutions takes reasonable steps to ensure that any collaborating organizations adhere to confidentiality and privacy obligations to protect your personal information.

Use of Your Personal Information

For each visitor to our site, we collect non-personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Browser type, version, and language.
  • Operating system.
  • Pages viewed while browsing the site.
  • Access times and referring website addresses.

This information is used internally to understand visitor traffic, analyze trends, and deliver personalized content.

Occasionally, we may use customer information for purposes not previously disclosed in our privacy notice. If our information practices change, data collected after the policy change will adhere to our updated practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

AppInnovate Solutions reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy at any time. If you object to the Privacy Policy, you should refrain from accessing or using our site.

Accessing Your Personal Information

You have the right to access your personal information, subject to exceptions allowed by law. If you would like to access your information, please contact us. For security purposes, we may require a written request, and a fee may apply for searching and providing access to your information on a per-request basis.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer’s hard drive through your web browser when you visit a website. These files are used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide insights to website owners.

You can disable cookies manually at any time—refer to your browser's ‘Help’ section for guidance. Disabling cookies will not affect your ability to view our site. However, to take full advantage of our website features, we recommend enabling cookies.
